AltFantasy Sports

AltFantasy Sports is a fantasy sports platform launched in 2019 to support season long fantasy football for the new (at the time) spring American football league Alliance of American Football. After gaining a sizable user base for that truncated season, the site expanded to support fantasy for more sports leagues that the major platforms did not support. The site is currently running strong and has tens of thousands of users that play fantasy sports in the spring and summer. It currently supports UFL, several Arena football leagues, UFA (ultimate frisbee), and CFL. New leagues are launching each year. 2025 will be the year of lacrosse and cricket.
